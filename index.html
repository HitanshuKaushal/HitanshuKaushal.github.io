Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions to help you find the information you need quickly.

How do I schedule a lab test appointment?

You can schedule a lab test appointment online through our website or by calling our office at 4377777890

Do I need a referral or prescription for lab tests?

Yes, most lab tests require a referral or prescription from your doctor. Please bring this document with you to your appointment.

What should I bring to my lab test appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and any referral or prescription from your doctor. Additionally, follow any specific preparation instructions provided.

How much do lab tests cost?

The cost of lab tests varies depending on the type of test and your insurance coverage. Please contact our billing department for specific pricing information.

Can I walk in for a lab test without an appointment?

Walk-in appointments are accepted based on availability. However, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure timely service.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please check with our office or your insurance provider to confirm coverage for your specific tests.
